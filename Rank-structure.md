# 📢 Alliance Rank Structure
# R5 – Alliance Leader
Leads the alliance and makes major decisions.
# R4 – Management 
Supports the leader with alliance management, recruitment, coordination, and member development.
# R3 – Veterans
Experienced players who can lead rallies, support members, and help during events and Period Attack battles.
# R2 – Members
Active players who are learning, growing, and contributing to the alliance.
# R1 – Recruits
Members who are new or have been inactive for more than 2 days. Continued inactivity may result in removal to make room for active players.

Let’s stay active, help each other grow, and keep strengthening the alliance together! 💪
