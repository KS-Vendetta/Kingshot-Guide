# 👑 Alliance Rank Structure

Our ranks are based on **activity, responsibility, and contribution**. Every role is important in helping our alliance grow stronger together.

---

## 👑 R5 — Alliance Leader
- Leads the alliance and sets its direction.
- Makes major alliance decisions.
- Oversees strategy and diplomacy.

---

## 🛡️ R4 — Leadership Team
- Supports the Alliance Leader.
- Assists with recruitment, member management, event coordination, and alliance development.
- Helps maintain a positive and active community.

---

## ⚔️ R3 — Veterans
- Experienced and trusted members.
- Lead rallies during Bear Trap and other alliance events.
- Guide newer members and provide gameplay support.

---

## 🤝 R2 — Members
- Active members who participate in alliance events.
- Continue growing, learning, and contributing to the alliance.
- Join rallies and support alliance activities.

---

## 🌱 R1 — Recruits
- New members getting familiar with the alliance.
- Members inactive for more than **2 days** may be moved to R1.
- Continued inactivity without notice may result in removal to make room for active players.

---

## 💙 Our Goal

Every rank plays an important role in our success. Stay active, support one another, and let's continue building a stronger alliance together! 💪
