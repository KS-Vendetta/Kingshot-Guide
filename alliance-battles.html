⚔️ Alliance Battle

Sanctuaries • Fortresses • Outposts

Alliance Battles are large-scale PvP events where teamwork, coordination and strategy are far more important than individual power. Winning these battles strengthens both you and the alliance.

🏰 Why Alliance Battles Matter

  • 🏆 Valuable personal and alliance rewards.
  • 💎 Alliance Honor and event rewards.
  • 📈 Strategic buffs and map control.
  • 👑 Increased alliance influence across the kingdom.
  • 💀 PvP experience and battle practice.
  • 🤝 Stronger teamwork and rally coordination.
Every member's participation contributes to the alliance's success.

⚔️ Attacking

Use your best three heroes and focus on offensive hero combinations.

🤝 Rally Join Heroes

Use one of these heroes as your first (left) hero:

  • Chenko — Lethality %
  • Yeonwoo — Lethality %
  • Amane — Attack %
  • Amadeus — Lethality %

If you don't own any of these heroes, join without heroes.

❤️ Healing Troops

  • Heal troops immediately after battles.
  • Heal in small batches (under 500 troops).
  • Request Alliance Help every time.
  • Repeat until all troops are fully healed.

⚠️ Battle Guidelines

  • 📍 Teleport near the battle only when instructed by R5 or R4.
  • 🏕️ Move into alliance territory before the battle begins.
  • 🚩 Leave the closest teleport spots for designated rally leaders.
  • ⚔️ Rally leaders will be assigned before each battle.
  • 🤝 Everyone else should focus on joining rallies quickly.
  • 🛡️ Use Level 7 troops or higher whenever possible.
  • 🦸 Always use the recommended heroes.
  • 🔄 If removed from a rally because of incorrect heroes, simply update your march and rejoin.
  • 🚫 Do not launch solo attacks unless instructed.
  • 🎯 Follow all R5/R4 instructions throughout the battle.
💙 Teamwork Wins Battles

A strong alliance isn't built by individual power—it's built by teamwork, communication and coordination.
