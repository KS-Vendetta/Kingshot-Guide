# 📦 Alliance Mobilization Complete missions. Earn points. Unlock rewards. Alliance Mobilization is a team event where members complete missions to earn points for themselves and the alliance. The more points earned, the better the milestone rewards for everyone. --- # 📋 Before the Event ✅ Save Construction, Research and Training Speedups. ✅ Delay completing long Construction or Research until the event begins. ✅ Keep enough resources available to complete missions. ✅ Refresh low-value missions and focus on the highest-point tasks. --- # ⭐ Quick Tips 🎯 Complete high-value missions. 🔄 Refresh poor-value missions. ⏳ Save speedups for the event. 🏗️ Time your research and construction. 🏆 Reach all personal milestones. 🤝 Every member should contribute. --- # 🎁 Rewards ## Personal Rewards By completing missions and reaching personal milestones, you can earn: - ⏱️ Speedups - 💎 Gems - 🪵 Resources - 🧱 Building Materials - 🦸 Hero Upgrade Materials - 📦 Personal Milestone Chests - 🏅 Event Ranking Rewards ## Alliance Rewards As the alliance reaches milestone targets, all participating members can receive: - 📦 Alliance Milestone Chests - ⏱️ Speedups - 💎 Gems - 🪵 Resources - 🦸 Hero Upgrade Materials - Additional event rewards The more members contribute, the better the rewards for everyone. --- # 📋 Best Mission Types Prioritize missions that provide the highest points for the least effort. Recommended missions: - 🏗️ Construction - 🔬 Research - ⚔️ Troop Training - 🌲 Resource Gathering - 🐻 Beast Hunting ⚠️ Refresh missions that require excessive resources for very few points. --- # 🚫 Common Mistakes ❌ Completing Construction or Research before the event starts. ❌ Accepting low-point missions. ❌ Letting missions expire. ❌ Using speedups on poor-value tasks. ❌ Ignoring easy gathering missions. --- # 💙 VEN Corner 💡 Save your Construction, Research and Training speedups specifically for Alliance Mobilization whenever possible. 💡 If you're unsure whether a mission is worth completing, ask an R4 before spending resources or speedups. 💡 If you're competing on the leaderboard, consider saving your speedups for the final hours to make a late push. 💡 Even if you can't reach the top rankings, completing your personal milestones helps the entire alliance unlock better rewards.
