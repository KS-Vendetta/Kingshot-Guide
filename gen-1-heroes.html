🦸 Generation 1 Heroes

Heroes are the backbone of every march. Choosing the right heroes and investing your resources wisely will significantly improve your performance in every event.

💡 F2P Golden Rule

Invest almost everything into your Top 3 heroes.

Focus your Hero EXP, Skill Books, Enhancement Materials, Scrolls and Forge Levels on: 🥇 Howard • 🥇 Saul • 🥇 Jabel

Secondary heroes should generally remain at Level 0–1 Gear until your primary march is complete.

💙 VEN Tips

  • ⭐ Focus on Howard, Saul and Jabel before investing in other heroes.
  • 📚 Save Skill Books for S-Tier heroes.
  • ⚒️ Keep secondary heroes at Level 0–1 gear until your main march is complete.
  • 🤝 Hero synergy and proper skills are more valuable than simply increasing power.
Build one exceptional march before building several average ones.
