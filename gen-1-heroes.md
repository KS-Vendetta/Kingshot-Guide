# 🦸 Hero Guide (Generation 1)

Heroes are the backbone of every march. Choosing the right heroes and investing your resources wisely will significantly improve your performance in every event.

---

# 💡 F2P Golden Rule

⚠️ **Focus all Hero EXP, Skill Books, Enhancement Materials, Scrolls, and Forge Levels on your Top 3 heroes only.**

For most F2P players, these should be:

🥇 **Howard** • 🥇 **Saul** • 🥇 **Jabel**

Secondary heroes should only wear **Level 0–1 gear** until your main heroes are fully developed.

# 💙 VEN Corner

- ⭐ Focus on **Howard, Saul, and Jabel** before investing in other heroes.
- 📚 Save Skill Books for **S-Tier heroes**.
- ⚒️ Keep secondary heroes at **Level 0–1 gear** until your main march is complete.
- 🤝 Hero synergy and the correct skills are more valuable than simply increasing power.
